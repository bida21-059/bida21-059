9 Bullets

A former burlesque dancer turned author (Lena Headey) discovers a second chance at life and redemption when she risks everything to save her young neighbor after he witnesses his parents' murder. Now on the run from the local crime boss (Sam Worthington), who also happens to be her longtime ex, she makes a last-ditch attempt to save the boy.
